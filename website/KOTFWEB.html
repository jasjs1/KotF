ABOUT THE GAME

Welcome to KINGDOM OF THE FALLEN: I, an epic medieval RPG where you forge your destiny in a war-torn land. Battle fearsome foes, unite kingdoms, and uncover ancient secrets hidden within the ruins of the fallen empire.

Game Features

Experience a deep and immersive RPG with dynamic gameplay:

  • Vast open world filled with dungeons, villages, and castles.
  • Customizable characters with skill trees and unique abilities.
  • Turn-based and real-time combat options.
  • Compelling storyline with multiple endings.
  • Rich lore and interactive NPCs.
